Negro Hanging to Tree.
Newport News, Va., June 10.- The dead
body of an unknown negro was found
hanging to a tree on the Brierfield road
near the city today. Investigation by a
coroner's jury tonight disclosed the fact
that the negro had been twice hanged.
He was first lynched or murdered by be
ing strung with a rope and his neck was
broken by the fall. The corpse was then
carried to the tree on which the body
was found, strap being drawn tightly
about his neck a.
