TWO NEGROES LYNCHED.
They Burned a Man's Barn and Several
Head of Stock.
Macon, Miss., February 18.- Two negroes
were lynched about six miles east
of this place last night. About two
months ago, L. F. Cole, living about six
miles from Macon, had his barn and several
head of stock burned. Parties have
been working on the case since, which
culmniated Saturday In shooting to
death Ishman and Henry Fed, two negroes
of the neighborhood. It is considered
that the evidence against them was
conclusive and no further is expected
