NEGRO LYNCHED.
He Was Suspected of Having Burned
a Barn Near Athens.
Athens, Ala., June 16.- Joe Harris, a
negro, was hanged by a mob in the
northern part of this( Limestone) County,
last night. Harris was suspected of
burning the barn of the farmer for whom
he worked, his grievance being against
fellow farm hand, who had stock
a housed in the barn destroyed.
